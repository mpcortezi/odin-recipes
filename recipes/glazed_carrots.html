Glazed Carrots

A simple side dish using just five basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Â¼ cup butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Seasonings
    • Salt
    • Ground white pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the carrots:Place the peeled and cut carrots in a saucepan, cover with water, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer until the carrots are tender. Drain.
  2. Glaze the carrots:Melt the butter in a saucepan, then add the sugar, salt and pepper. Add the carrots and toss until well-coated.
